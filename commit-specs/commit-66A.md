## Background

C66 has 4 planned product files (the `max_changed_files` cap). The lifecycle hook
auto-generates `.context/direct/C66.md`, which passes the `planned` check (added by
`check_actual_scope`) but still counts toward the total `changed_files` tally — pushing
it to 5 and failing the cap. Previous Claude-direct commits had fewer planned files so
never hit this. The `.context/direct/CNN.md` brief is always generated by the lifecycle
hook — it is not implementation output and should be excluded like other runtime
artifacts.
